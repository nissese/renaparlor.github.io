More on the Author

I was born in January, 1960, in Magamaga, Kagoma Parish, Jinja District. After Primary and Secondary School, I studied at Nakawa Vocational Training Institute a Secretarial Course. In the 80's, I, worked with Ministry of Planning and Economic Development, as personal secretary. Thereafter I joined UNDP, at the Headquarters in Kampala. Then I worked as a personal secretary, on different UNDP Rehabilitation projects based in Entebbe.

In the early 90's I went abroad, lived in Germany for 26 Years. In German studied Export Trade and Regulations. Among others I worked with STEADTLER Mars GmbH & Co a Producer of all types of writing, drawing, colouring, modelling material, Instruments and colours. Worked 15 Years with Siemens AG, Digital Factory Sector which offers integrated hardware, software and technology-based services to support manufacturing companies worldwide. In both companies was employed in the export department.
